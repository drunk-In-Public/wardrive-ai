📖 How to Use

1. Enter your WiGLE API credentials above and tap Test Connection

2. Pan the map to your target area (city block to neighborhood scale works best)

3. Tap Scan & Plan Route — the AI will fetch existing scan data and find areas with low coverage

4. Review the color-coded grid: green = unscanned, red = already dense

5. The blue route connects the best cold spots in driving order

6. Tap 📥 GPX to export to any GPS app (Organic Maps, Maps.me, Garmin, etc.)

7. For iPhone: in Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen to install as an app
